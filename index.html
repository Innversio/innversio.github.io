About Us

Our experts bring tougether a deep understanding of product requirements, latest methodologies, , advanced techniques and materials, customer use specifications and costing objectives to accompany you through the entire design and development process. We also help businesses solve their mechanical problems through on-site visits to have a better understanding, and comprehensively reverse engineer our way through the problem. We are open to all kinds of projects and will provide a fresh perspective, may it be building something from scratch or making changes to existing designs.

Services

Mechanical Design

We can make designs that are mechanically feasible via mathematical calculations and modelling. We will provide you with apt technical drawings and documentation as per requirements.

Product Design

We can help you realize your ideas from a rough problem statement and give it shape. Whether the idea is a concept, a design on paper or an initial iteration, we’ll try to make it better. We can provide you with sound 3D models that work.

Realistic Renders

We can generate photorealistic and non-photorealistic images from 2D and 3D models. They can help with presentations and help in better understanding of the designs.

Product Animation

With advanced computer tools, we can create working animations and exploded animations for assemblies for technical illustrations. It also illustrates the working of all components with a better perspective.

Prototyping

From initial design, our design team can implement ideas into tangible forms from paper to digital. We can build prototypes of varying degrees of fidelity to capture design concepts, validation and testing. You can refine the designs so that the final products are sustainable.

Manufacturing

We have access to all kinds of machines and have many associates with wide ranging services. They include casting, machining, injection moulding, joining, painting, pasting, additive manufacturing and many more.

3D Printing

Additive manufacturing is advantageous because of flexible designs, rapid prototyping and can be accurate and cost-effective method of prototyping. We can provide scaled and working models of various designs to help understand the designs better. ABS, ABS PC, flexible materials of multiple colors and surface finish.

CAD

To aid in the creation, modification, analysis, or optimization of a design, we can create 2D and 3D models depending upon the problem statement. A vague statement might be enough for ideation and CAD.

FEA

We have an expertise in FEA simulations which enhance productivity and profits by providing early evaluation of critical design parameters. We are proficient in Linear Static Analysis, Nonlinear Analysis, Dynamic Analysis, Modal Analysis, Thermal Analysis and Fatigue Analysis.

CFD

The systems having flow of liquid and gases with defined boundary conditions can be simulated using computational simulations. We can study the flow patterns, heat transfer, aerodynamic drag and various relevant parameters.

MBD

Multiple bodies in a system consisting of solid bodies or links connected via joints can be simulated in multi-body dynamics. We can simulate the movement of these systems and study their behavior under the influence of forces.

Optimization

We can optimize the material layouts and shapes within a defined design space, for a given set of boundary conditions and constraints with various target parameters. We can optimize the weight, stiffness to weight ratio and displacement.

Reliability Engineering

We can provide you with period of time a system can function under stated conditions using computational and mathematical calculations. It can ease the maintenance and enhance cost-effectiveness.

About Us

We help businesses solve their mechanical problems through on-site visits to have a better understanding, and comprehensively reverse engineer our way through the problem. We are open to any kind of challenge, may it be building something from scratch or making changes to an existing design. Our hands on experience on designing and manufacturing gives us an edge over many other people and provides a different and a fresh perspective. We are young minds with a newer understanding of the field and that might just be the thing you are looking for.
